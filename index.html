My BIG idea

Coming up with a big idea wasn’t something I was planning on so early in this course, I truly didn’t know if I was confident enough to come up with such an answer, but I took a step back and asked myself what would I enjoy making, and just like everything else in my life, the answer was those Gosh Dang Vidya Games, that’ll rot my brain and turn my eyes square. So, I decided my BIG idea would actually be a little puzzle game.

I have spent the majority of my life playing video games and want to actively work in the industry so the idea of making a video game isn’t by any means a foreign or unwanted thought, in fact independently making a small indie puzzle game would be a great display of talent, test of skill and a great material achievement to display on a resume. The video game industry made over $162.32 Billion in 2020 alone, that is plenty of evidence that there is a demand for them, especially with that whole global pandemic going on at the moment. I hope that the pandemic isn’t a factor by the time I enter the industry, but my point was that the growth the industry saw in 2020 will have a permanent effect in the long term

The product I wish to make would be an isometric, 2D, puzzle game hopefully made via the use of Unity. The product would offer a light hearted short story told through the progression of the player character using environmental movement and basic pairing skills to solve short puzzles. it would provide a challenge through resource management (in this case limiting the amount of movement the player character can perform), elemental pairing (using certain in game elements and making them necessary to activate specific tools in the environment), and to use level and layout design to create intuitive and restricting environmental traversal. It would be a free to play game with no additional transactions. this would encourage the purchase of the game without the worry of any long term commitment on the part of the consumer, that other free to play games suffer from. The game would provide a much wanted service to the people, entertainment, one of the most sought after luxuries in modern life.
